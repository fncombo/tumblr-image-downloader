Clear list of downloaded images

Tumblr Image Downloader automatically remembers all the images you have downloaded and puts a tick ✓ next to the download button.

This will permanently forget all the images you have downloaded so far.

Confirm downloading again

Toggle whether to bring up a confirmation dialog when downloading an already downloaded image. This will affect the settings on any currently open pages.
